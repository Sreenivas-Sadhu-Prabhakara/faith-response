Drum-Buffer-Rope (DBR) Methodology

We apply the Theory of Constraints to HIA's security checkpoints. The "Drum" (Checkpoint capacity) dictates the flow, while the Felize Bracelet acts as the "Rope," timing passenger movement to eliminate terminal congestion.

AODB Integration

Real-time synchronization with the Airport Operational Database allows for immediate response to flight delays or gate changes.
